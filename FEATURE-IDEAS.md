# De Xuat Tinh Nang

Tai lieu nay gom nhung tinh nang nen can nhac them cho Memory Tree.

## 1. Cai app len dien thoai ro hon

- Hien popup huong dan rieng cho Android va iPhone.
- Them man hinh onboarding sau khi cai app lan dau.
- Cho phep tai anh icon va splash screen dep hon cho PWA.

## 2. Nhat ky theo ngay va theo moc su kien

- Loc ky niem theo nam, thang, nguoi tao.
- Gom ky niem theo su kien lon: du lich, cuoi, ky niem gia dinh.
- Them tim kiem full-text cho tieu de va noi dung.

## 3. Chia se va hop tac tot hon

- Moi thanh vien bang link chia se nhanh.
- Cho phep gan vai tro: owner, editor, viewer.
- Hien lich su ai vua them hay sua ky niem nao.

## 4. Media dep hon

- Tu tao thumbnail cho video.
- Gallery fullscreen cho anh/video.
- Nen anh truoc khi upload de tang toc tren dien thoai.

## 5. Tree UI thong minh hon

- Zoom nhe va auto-focus vao ky niem vua them.
- Chuyen doi giua che do cay va che do album doc.
- Danh dau ky niem noi bat bang sticker hoac mau sac dac biet.

## 6. Nhac nho va thong bao

- Nhac lai ky niem vao dung ngay trong nam.
- Push notification khi co thanh vien moi them ky niem.
- Widget nho de mo nhanh ky niem gan day.

## 7. Sao luu va an toan du lieu

- Export toan bo ky niem ra ZIP hoac JSON.
- Import lai tu file backup.
- Trang quan ly dung luong media da dung.
